# BYLROS Customer Portal Guide

Welcome to the BYLROS Customer Portal! This guide will help you access and use your customer account.

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Portal

There are two ways to access the customer portal:

1. **Direct URL**: Navigate to your BYLROS portal URL and add `/customer` to the path
   - Example: `https://your-bylros-portal.com/customer`

2. **From Admin Login**: On the admin login page, click the "Customer Portal" link at the bottom

## Creating Your Account

Before you can create a portal account, you must be an existing BYLROS customer with a record in the system.

### Step-by-Step Sign Up

1. **Click** "New customer? Create an account"

2. **Enter Your Email**
   - Use the same email that BYLROS has on file for you
   - This email must match your customer record

3. **Enter Your Phone Number**
   - Use the same phone number registered with BYLROS
   - Format: +971 XX XXX XXXX (or your country format)

4. **Create a Password**
   - Minimum 6 characters
   - Choose a secure password

5. **Confirm Your Password**
   - Re-enter the same password

6. **Click** "Create Account"

7. **Success!** You'll see a green message confirming your account was created

### Important Notes

- Your email AND phone number must match what BYLROS has on file
- If you get an error, contact BYLROS support to verify your information
- Once created, you can sign in immediately

## Signing In

1. Enter your email address
2. Enter your password
3. Click "Sign In"
4. You'll be taken to your dashboard

## Dashboard Features

### Overview Tab

Your main dashboard shows:

- **Quotes Received**: Total number of quotes you've received
- **Active Orders**: Number of current orders
- **Site Visits**: Scheduled visits
- **Invoices**: Total invoices
- **Total Value**: Sum of all your invoices in AED

Quick action buttons:
- **Track Orders**: Jump to order tracking
- **View Invoices**: See your invoice history

### Profile Tab

View and update your information:

- Full Name
- Email Address
- Phone Number
- Location/Address
- Customer Status (Lead, Quoted, Ordered, Delivered, Installed)

**To Edit Your Profile:**
1. Click "Edit Profile"
2. Update any fields
3. Click "Save Changes"
4. Or click "Cancel" to discard changes

### Quotes Tab

View all quotes you've received from BYLROS:

- Quote number and date
- Total amount in AED
- Status (Draft, Sent, Accepted, Rejected)
- Subtotal, VAT, and number of items
- Remarks from BYLROS
- Validity date

**Actions:**
- Click "Download PDF" to get a professional quote document with BYLROS branding

### Orders Tab

Track your orders in real-time:

- Order number and placement date
- Current status with visual progress bar
- Progress indicators: Confirmed → In Production → Delivered → Installed
- Order date
- Delivery date (if scheduled)
- Installation date (if scheduled)

**Order Statuses:**
- **Confirmed**: Order has been confirmed
- **In Production**: Currently being manufactured
- **Delivered**: Product has been delivered
- **Installed**: Installation completed

### Invoices Tab

Manage your invoices and payments:

- Invoice number and date
- Related order number
- Payment progress bar
- Total amount
- Deposit paid
- Payment before delivery
- Remaining balance
- Payment status (Unpaid, Partial, Paid)
- Due date
- Payment history with dates and methods

**Actions:**
- Click "Download Invoice PDF" for a complete invoice with payment history

### Site Visits Tab

See all your scheduled and past site visits:

**Upcoming Visits** (highlighted in blue):
- Date and time
- Location
- Status
- Remarks
- Payment information (if required)

**Past Visits**:
- Completed visits
- Cancelled visits
- Historical record

## Tips for Best Experience

1. **Keep Your Profile Updated**: Ensure your contact information is current
2. **Check Regularly**: New quotes and updates appear automatically
3. **Download PDFs**: Keep copies of quotes and invoices for your records
4. **Contact BYLROS**: If you see any discrepancies, contact support immediately
5. **Sign Out**: Always sign out when done, especially on shared devices

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Create an Account?

**Problem**: "No customer record found with this phone and email"

**Solution**:
- Verify you're using the exact email and phone number BYLROS has on file
- Contact BYLROS support to confirm your information
- Ask them to add or update your customer record

### Forgot Password?

Currently, password reset is handled through Supabase. Contact BYLROS support for assistance.

### Not Seeing Your Data?

If quotes, orders, or invoices are missing:
1. Verify you're signed in with the correct account
2. Contact BYLROS support to confirm data has been entered
3. Try signing out and back in

### Technical Issues?

For any technical problems:
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear your browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Contact BYLROS support

## Security & Privacy

- Your data is protected with Row Level Security
- You can only see your own information
- All connections are encrypted
- Never share your password
- Always sign out on shared devices

# Quick Start Guide - BYLROS Platform

## Step 1: Create Your First Admin Account

1. Open the application in your browser
2. You'll see the login page with the BYLROS logo
3. Click the link at the bottom: **"Don't have an account? Create one"**
4. Fill in the sign-up form:
   - **Email**: Enter your email (e.g., `admin@bylros.ae`)
   - **Password**: Create a strong password (minimum 6 characters)
   - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter the same password
5. Click **"Create Account"**
6. You'll see a green success message: "Account created successfully! You can now sign in."

## Step 2: Sign In

1. The form will automatically switch back to sign-in mode
2. Enter the email and password you just created
3. Click **"Sign In"**
4. You're now in the BYLROS platform dashboard!

## Step 3: Start Using the Platform

### Add Your First Customer
1. Click **"Customers"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"Add Customer"** button
3. Fill in:
   - Name
   - Phone number
   - Email (optional)
   - Location (optional)
   - Status (starts as "Lead")
   - Notes (optional)
4. Click **"Save"**

### Create Your First Quote
1. Click **"Quotes"** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Create Quote"**
3. Select a customer from the dropdown
4. Add line items by clicking **"+ Add Item"**:
   - Location (e.g., "Living Room")
   - Type (e.g., "Window", "Sliding Door")
   - Height in cm (e.g., 200)
   - Width in cm (e.g., 150)
   - Quantity (e.g., 2)
   - Unit Price (AED per m²)
5. Watch the area and totals calculate automatically
6. Add remarks if needed
7. Click **"Save Quote"**
8. Export to PDF or Excel using the buttons

### Schedule a Site Visit
1. Click **"Site Visits"** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Schedule Visit"**
3. Select a customer
4. Choose date and time
5. Enter the visit location
6. Add remarks if needed
7. Click **"Schedule"**

### Track Orders
1. Click **"Order Tracker"** in the sidebar
2. This is the public-facing page where customers can track their orders
3. Enter a customer's phone number or email
4. Click **"Track Order"**
5. View all orders with progress bars showing status

## Step 4: Switch Languages

1. Look at the bottom of the sidebar
2. Click on the language dropdown
3. Select:
   - **English**
   - **العربية** (Arabic with RTL layout)
   - **中文** (Chinese)

## Tips

- All monetary amounts are in **AED** (UAE Dirham)
- VAT is automatically calculated at **5%**
- PDF exports include full BYLROS branding
- Customer status follows the journey: Lead → Quoted → Ordered → Delivered → Installed
- Use the Dashboard to see real-time statistics

## Need Help?

- Check the main README.md for detailed documentation
- All data is securely stored in Supabase
- Your session will remain active until you sign out
